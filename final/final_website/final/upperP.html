Let Up North Planner find your new vacation in the UP!

The Upper Peninsula has so much beauty to be explored. Take a look at some of the most popular tourist destinations!

Copper Harbor


  • Location: The very most northern point of the UP
  • Great for: hiking, kayaking, exploring
  • For those who enjoy a rustic, small town experience
  • Things to see: Northern lights, Lake Superior
  • Known for: Tip of Michigan and Jack Pine Forests

Pictured Rocks Lakeshore


  • Location: Munising
  • Great for: hiking, kayaking, exploring,
  • For those who enjoy a rustic, small town experience
  • Things to see: waterfalls, lakeshore, Lake Superior
  • Known for: waterfalls, mining, lighthouses

Tahquamenon Falls


  • Location: Central UP
  • Great for: hiking and photography
  • For those who enjoy landmarks and nature
  • Things to see: The waterfall in the Spring time
  • Known for: Michigan's largest waterfall
