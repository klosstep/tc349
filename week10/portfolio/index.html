What you need to know about Stephen Klos

Born in Ann Arbor, residing in East Lansing.

Stephen Klos is a junior enrolled at Michigan State University majoring in Media and Information. He is currently working as a student Web Developer at The State News. After graduation in 2016, Stephen plans to become a full time Web Developer in the Metro Detroit area.

It all began senior year of high school when a friend recommended to take an introductory interactive media course. After learning some basic HTML and CSS using Dreamweaver, he began to develop a passion for graphic design and programming. He earned a certification of competency in web design and realized that Web Design and Development was a very practical career. He has pursued it ever since.

In his free time, Stephen loves shooting and editing photos with his Canon T3i. Some of his recent photography includes the idea of trying to bring artificial objects to real life by using that object in real and relevant background. Mountain biking, badminton, snowboarding, fantasy football, lawn sports, the Red Wings, Halo matchmaking, robe wearing and onion ring eating are some of his other interests.

If you wish to keep up with Stephen, you can follow him on the links on the bottom of the page!
