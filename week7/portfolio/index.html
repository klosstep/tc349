The Media Collection of Stephen Klos

Born in Ann Arbor, residing in East Lansing.

Stephen Klos is currently enrolled at Michigan State University majoring in Media and Information. He is currently working as a student Web Developer at The State News. He is currently a junior, and plans to graduate in May 2016. After graduation Stephen plans to become a full time Web Developer in the Metro Detroit area.

Stephen found his passions for web design his senior year of high school when a friend recommended to take an introductory media course. He realized it was a practical career and has pursued it ever since.
